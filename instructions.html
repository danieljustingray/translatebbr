How to use this translator

This translator has been made to be very easy to use.

  • Do not type contractions (instead of say I'm say I am, saves me typing time)
  • When typing plural words, type "word -s". For example "Beds" becomes "Bed -s" and the outpout would be "lit -ze". Plural words in Burenburgish have a -ze as suffix.
